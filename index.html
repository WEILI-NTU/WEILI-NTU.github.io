About Me

I am currently a Research Fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, working with Prof. Chau Yuen. I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) in 2023.

Research Interests

My research interests mainly focus on 6G wireless communications, including:

  • Channel estimation and channel modeling
  • Beamforming design
  • Electromagnetic information theory for emerging wireless communications
  • Quantum computation
